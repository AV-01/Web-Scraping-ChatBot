WELCOME TO THE ROCKLIN HIGH SCHOOL COLLEGE AND CAREER CENTER
The College and Career Center is located to the right of the main library entrance off the amphitheatre in the center of campus. On this website you will find lots of information about career exploration and college planning. If you do not see what you are looking for, please contact Rachel Lund in the College and Career Center: rlund@rocklinusd.org
 
Walk-in hours: Monday - Friday during Brunch/Break and Lunch
